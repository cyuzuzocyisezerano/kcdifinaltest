Vocational Training

Empowering individuals with practical skills for a brighter future.

Why Vocational Training?

At **Koroboza Career Development Center (KCDC)**, we believe that vocational training is the cornerstone of self-sufficiency and economic growth. Our programs equip individuals with the hands-on skills needed to succeed in the workforce, start their own businesses, and contribute to their communities. We offer a variety of training programs tailored to meet the needs of today’s job market.

Our Training Programs

Tailoring & Fashion Design

Learn the art of tailoring and fashion design. Our program covers everything from basic sewing skills to advanced techniques in garment construction, pattern making, and fashion illustration. With hands-on training, you'll be prepared to start your own business or work in the fashion industry.

Carpentry & Woodworking

Master the craft of carpentry and woodworking. From furniture making to construction, our program teaches the technical skills needed to succeed in this ever-demanding trade. Gain expertise in handling tools, measuring, and building structures that last.

Hairdressing & Beauty Therapy

Dive into the world of beauty and hair care. Our hairdressing program includes everything from basic haircuts to advanced styling and color techniques. Learn to manage a beauty salon, practice skincare treatments, and create lasting impressions on your clients.

Agriculture & Farming

Our agricultural training program offers practical skills in farming, gardening, and animal husbandry. Learn how to manage a farm, grow organic crops, and care for livestock. With sustainable practices at the core, you’ll be equipped to succeed in the agriculture sector.

ICT & Digital Skills

In today’s digital age, technology is vital. Our ICT and digital skills program includes web development, coding, graphic design, and office software applications. Gain valuable skills that open up opportunities in various sectors, from tech startups to corporate offices.
